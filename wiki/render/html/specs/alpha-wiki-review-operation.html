Alpha-Wiki Review Operation

Provenance

  • Source: commands/review.md.
  • Source: skills/review/SKILL.md.
  • Tool source: tools/review.py.

Entities

  • Operation: `/alpha-wiki:review` / `$alpha-wiki-review`.
  • Review inputs: status snapshot, lint findings, graph semantics, provenance, freshness, open questions.
  • Review output: blockers, warnings, structural findings, and next maintenance actions.

Requirements

  • Review audits wiki memory quality, not AgentOps team process.
  • It must answer whether the wiki is coherent, current, navigable, and safe for an agent to rely on.
  • It checks stale pages, orphan services/modules/contracts, actionable open questions, contract owner/consumer links, decision links, and graph/markdown alignment.
  • Missing knowledge routes to [[alpha-wiki-ingest-operation]].
  • Schema gaps route to evolve.

Gates

  • Do not invent content during review.
  • Every suggested action maps to a command or file.
  • Saved reports must be suitable for CI/GitHub issue bodies when generated by automation.
